The world of Fel has fractured with the sudden return of magic previously lost to time. Overnight, some awakened with the power to perform feats once thought impossible. Citizen-led revolts toppled empires that couldn't defend themselves, entire cities were wiped from the map thanks to awakened artifacts, and monsters enchanted by newfound magic roamed the lands. Fel had fallen into chaos.

*The Cataclysm of Fel* follows five disparate individuals shaped by this cataclysmic event, Thrown together aboard a train bound for the Azure Coast, they quickly discover that making your way in this new era of Fel is not for the weak. Out of necessity, they come together as a group, searching for answers on their own pasts, and finding meaning when the impossible has become real.
